Shri Venkanath Vidyalaya Loni Venkanath.




Sri Venkanath Vidyalaya Lonivenkanath was established in 1986 and is managed by Prof. helped It is in a rural area. It is located in Srigonda block in Ahmednagar district of Maharashtra. The school has grades 5 to 12. The school is co-educational and does not have a pre-primary department attached to it. The nature of the school is unanswered and the school building is not used as a shift-school. Marathi is the medium of instruction in this school.This school is accessible by all weather road. The academic session of this school starts in April. The school has a private building. It has 8 classrooms for education. All classrooms are in good condition.It has 2 other rooms for non-academic activities. The school has a separate room for the Principal/Teacher. The school has a solid boundary wall. The school has no electricity connection. The source of drinking water in the school is tap water and it is working. The school has 3 children's toilets and it is functioning.And 2 girls toilet and it is operational. The school has a playground. The school has a library and there are 3500 books in the library. The school does not require ramps for children with disabilities to enter the classroom. The school has 5 computers for teaching and learning and all are functional. There is no computer aided learning lab in the school. Mid-day meal is provided and prepared in the school premises.
