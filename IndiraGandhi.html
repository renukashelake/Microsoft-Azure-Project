Indira Gandhi Vidyaniketan Srigonda.




Indira Gandhi Vidyaniketan is located in Srigonda, Ahmednagar. 21 dedicated and professional teachers ensure that children get the most out of their education at this other board school. Indira Gandhi Vidya Niketan was started in 1981. As the student teacher ratio is 30:1 the primary medium of education is Marathi and other secondary medium. The school prides itself on its excellent education system.The school provides education to students from 6th to 10th standard. The current student strength of the school is approximately 633. There are 2681 books in the library of this school. The school has produced exceptional results in academics and students have also excelled in extra co-curricular activities.
