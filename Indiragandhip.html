Indira Gandhi Polytechnic Belwandi Sh.




Indira Gandhi Polytechnic Belwandi (Sugar) is located in Maharashtra. This educational institute was established in the year 1982. The college is affiliated to Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Mumbai. It is approved by All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE). Indira Gandhi Polytechnic Belwandi (Sugar) offers Diploma in Engineering courses.The college has well developed infrastructure and facilities like computer lab, auditorium, sports etc. The college has trained and experienced faculty members.
