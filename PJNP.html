Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru Vidyalaya Pimpalgaon Pisa.




Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru Vidyalaya Pimpalgaonpisa was established in 1983 and is managed by Prof. helped It is in a rural area. It is located in Srigonda block in Ahmednagar district of Maharashtra. The school has grades 5 to 10. The school is co-educational and does not have a pre-primary department attached to it. The nature of the school is unanswered and the school building is not used as a shift-school. Marathi is the medium of instruction in this school. This school is accessible by all weather road.The academic session of this school starts in April. The school building is on rent. It has 4 classrooms for education. All classrooms are in good condition. It has 2 other rooms for non-academic activities. The school has a separate room for the Principal/Teacher. The school has a barbed wire fence. The school has electricity connection. There is no source of drinking water in the school and it is functional. The school has 1 children's toilet which is functional. And 1 girls toilet and it is functional. The school has a playground. The school has a library and has 1978 books in the library.The school does not require ramps for children with disabilities to enter the classroom. The school has 5 computers for teaching and learning and all are functional. There is no computer aided learning lab in the school. Mid-day meal is provided and prepared in the school premises.
