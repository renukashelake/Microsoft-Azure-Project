One of Srigonda Taluka Wangdari is a village located at the end and on the banks of Ghodandi. I spent my childhood in this village. Bapuni primary there Studied. As there is no facility of secondary education in the village, I am staying at Malegaon (T. Baramati) now Studied secondary education. But unfortunately with the death of grandfather Shankarao (Tatya) Bapu left his education halfway Returned to the village.
